Oklahoma State Meal (One of them)

Hamburger with Fried Okra

This recipe is one of the state meals for Oklahoma. It consists of a hamburger with grilled onions and cheese with a side of breaded-fried okra.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Black Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Douse okra in milk
  2. Put okra in an equal parts flour and cornmeal mixture with black pepper to your liking
  3. Fry in oil until golden brown, when finished garnish with parsley
  4. Make burger with grilled onions and cheese of your choice
