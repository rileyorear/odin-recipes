Pasta with Meat sauce

This recipe consists of pasta with meat sauce. It is easy to make and it is filling.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles of your choice
  • Ground Beef
  • Prego pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in large pan until grease has boiled off
  2. Add Prego pasta sauce to ground beef pan and turn stove on low
  3. Wait for pastas to finish boiling
  4. Top with a grated cheese of your choice
